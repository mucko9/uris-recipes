Uri's Eggs Benedict

Learn how to make the best Eggs Benedict with Poached Eggs and easy Hollandaise Sauce on a toasted English muffin. This is a famous American dish and it’s a staple on restaurant breakfast menus.

Ingredients

  • Hollandaise Sauce
  • 2 English Muffins
  • Unsalted butter
  • Canadian Bacon
  • 1 Egg
  • Distilled white vinegar
  • Cayenne pepper, for garnish
  • Chives, for garnish

Steps

  1. Prep for Poached Eggs: Place a pot of water on the stove for making poached eggs and bring it to barely a simmer. Once water is barely simmering, poach 4 eggs
  2. Sauté meat: In a large skillet, sauté meat until warm and golden then transfer to a plate, cover to keep warm.
  3. Toast English muffins: Melt 2 tsp butter on the same skillet and add English muffins cut-side down. Toast until golden brown. Turn off the heat and leave muffins in the pan to keep warm.
  4. Place English muffin halves on serving plates, cut side up, and top with your meat. Place the egg over the meat. Pour the hollandaise sauce over the eggs, sprinkle the top with cayenne pepper and garnish with chives. Serve immediately.
